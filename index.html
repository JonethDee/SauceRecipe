Thai Dipping Sauce

This sauce is a staple for SouthEast Asian proteins. It pairs well with steak and seafood dishes. It is meant to be served family style or during a backyard BBQ. It has a nice spicy kick and fresh earthy taste with the optional addition of green onion and cilantro. This is one of the sauces I grew up with and will always enjoy.

Thai Dipping Sauce (Nam Prik)

Ingredients










Instructions
  1. Add the garlic cloves and thai chili into a mortar. Using the pestle, crush the galric and garlic until it looks like a paste or minced.
  2. Mince the cilantro and green onion finely. Then add it into the mortar.
  3. Pour some fish sauce and squeeze a lime into the mortar.
  4. Then add some MSG and Sugar.
  5. Lightly Mash all the ingredients together and mix it as you do.
  6. Pour into a small bowl and ENJOY!
